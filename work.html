PSWC™

CASE: Thousands of people fall victim of "the man" every day at work, with traditional industries like manufacturing being leader of unfair treatment of workers worldwide, PSWC aims to change this. Progressive Struggle of the Working Class (PSWC) is an organization committed to changing the lives of many workers in the manufacturing industry by negotiating fair wages and working hours, and also making sure that companies pay close attention to the worker's well-being.

APPROACH: Build a site where people can learn about the organization, the services offered and the ability to sign up for membership.
